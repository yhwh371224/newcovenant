Popular questions

The price you receive from EasyGo includes the airport parking fee, and we do not charge extra for any waiting time incurred due to flight delays. Our price is fixed, and there are no additional charges.

EasyGo drivers will keep track of your flights landing time in order to meet you on time. He will call or message you by the time you collect your baggage. Simply proceed to the meeting point, and your driver will be waiting for you there.

EasyGo accepts all major credit cards, including American Express, MasterCard, and VisaCard. Alternatively, you can pay cash to your driver on the day of your booking. However, for those arriving in Sydney, we kindly request payment in advance.

We do provide an infant seat or baby capsule, and two car seats are available free of charge. If you require more than two seats, we charge an extra $10 per additional seat. Please specify the number of seats you require during the booking process.

Our standard shuttle mini bus can accommodate up to thirteen (13) passengers. You can view a photo of the vehicle on our webpage.'EASYGO SERVICES'

EASYGO allows one (1) bag plus one (1) carry-on per person. If you have additional bags, please inform our office.
