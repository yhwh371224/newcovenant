Airport Meeting Points

International Terminal – T1

Public Pick-up , Please proceed to the P7 parking building first after exiting the arrival halls, and then follow the signs for 'Public Pick-up'

Please note: On landing simply turn on your mobile phone
The meeting point may vary by the notification of your driver on the flight day

Map of International airport Public Pickup & Rideshare


Domestic Terminal – T2

Please proceed to the Priority Pick-up (Area C) This is where you meet your driver

Please note: On landing simply turn on your mobile phone
The meeting point may vary by the notification of your driver on the flight day

Map of Priority Pick-up
Map of Shuttle bus bay


Qantas Terminal – T3

Inside the Qantas Terminal at the baggage carousel number one (1). This is where you meet your driver, who will then lead you out to the bus.

Please note: On landing simply turn on your mobile phone
